HISTORY

Founder: Max Sonnenberg | Founded: 1931 | Headquarters: Cape Town, South Africa

Woolworths started in 1931 when Max Sonnenberg opened the first store in Cape Town. The business introduced a non-negotiable pricing policy, meaning no bargaining, which was a new concept in South Africa at the time. It quickly became popular for offering high-quality goods at fair prices.
